Background

Goku was born on Planet Vegeta, long story short, they determined that he was too weak but the Saiyans still wanted to get their use out of him so they sent him to Earth to conquer the planet as a baby. However, when his pod landed on earth it he managed to hit his head in the process. This caused Goku to have amnesia. Due to that reason alone, it re-worked his entire thought process and allowed him to become what we know today through rigorous training with a multitude of different masters. Grandpa Gohan, Master Roshi, King Kai and the list goes on.

He not only got trained by some of the top masters around the universe he also had the opportunity and duty to upkeep Earths protection. In doing so, he has fought infamous people that were known to bring destruction or potentially going to bring destruction. To name a few major ones, he fought Frieza, Cell, and Majin Buu. There are a multitude of others he fought but those mentioned are some of the key players in Dragon Ball Z that he fought in order to protect Earth.

Now, some more major things that Goku achieved would be the numerous transformations that we've seen him reach. With these transformations came a new height in power for him which allowed him to continue his journey to protect earth. If you want a more detailed history I'd recommend that you check out this link. Below you will find information regarding the transformations that he achieved.

Transformations

This is Goku's first transformation that he achieves in the DBZ series. It is called Super Saiyan 1. This form comes with golden hair, and a golden aura. His eyes turn blue and his hair becomes spiky. Overall, his power and speed increases and in doing so he was able to defeat Frieza using this form.

This is Goku's second transformation. In this form, his hair becomes even spikier and it also seems as if he has grown more hair. His eyes stay blue and his hair and aura stay gold. Aesthetically, the largest change would be the fact that he now has lightning that arcs around his body. He used this form to attemp to defeat Majin Vegeta, but loses.

This is Goku's third and final transformation that we see in Dragon Ball Z. This is, by far, the most powerful of his transformations and by a long shot. Again, as before, he continues to have his blue eyes and gold hair. However, his hair is now down to the back of his knees. He uses this form to fight Majin Buu at one point in the DBZ series.
